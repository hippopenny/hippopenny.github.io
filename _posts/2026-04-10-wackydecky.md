**Wacky Decky: Card Merge** is what happens when **2048** gets a deck, a drag-and-drop attitude, and exactly zero chill. Two identical cards touch? Boom—bigger number, bigger brain, bigger "okay *one more* run" energy.

You are not "casually puzzling." You are **conducting a stack orchestra**. Merge from different piles, dodge the full-board nightmare, and chase that high score like it owes you money. Colorful art, smooth merges, and the kind of tactile feedback that makes you nod at your phone like it said something wise.

Are you really going to let a *card* outplay you? Download it. Prove it. Apologize to your battery later.
